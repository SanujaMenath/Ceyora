About Ceyora

Our Story

Ceyora was founded with a passion for preserving and sharing the rich cultural traditions of Sri Lanka. For over three generations, our family has been dedicated to supporting local artisans and craftspeople who create these magnificent pieces using techniques passed down through centuries. Each item in our collection tells a story of Sri Lankan heritage, from the intricate patterns of our handwoven textiles to the spiritual significance of our traditional masks. We work directly with skilled artisans across the island, ensuring fair trade practices and helping to sustain these ancient crafts for future generations. Our mission is to bring the beauty and cultural richness of Sri Lanka to the world, while supporting the communities that create these extraordinary works of art.
