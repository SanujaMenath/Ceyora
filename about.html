About Ceyora

Our Story

At Ceyora, we bring the beauty of Sri Lankan tradition to your hands. We carefully source and sell a wide range of authentic products including traditional clothing, handwoven cane items, handcrafted masks, and unique handmade jewelry. Each piece reflects the rich culture, artistry, and heritage of our island, made by skilled artisans who keep these traditions alive. Whether you are looking for something timeless to wear, a cultural piece to decorate your home, or a special handmade gift, our collection connects you with the true spirit of tradition.
